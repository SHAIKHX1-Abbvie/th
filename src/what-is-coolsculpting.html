What is CoolSculpting®?

The CoolSculpting® fat-freezing procedure is a non-surgical fat reduction treatment.*1 It uses controlled cooling to reduce that stubborn fat that’s difficult to shift.

Non-surgical7
No needles2,7
Reduces fat cells19
Expected 2 hours downtime20,21

CoolSculpting® technology

Procedure Safety

CoolSculpting® technology delivers controlled cooling to target the fat cells underneath the skin,2 and there is 2 hours expected downtime after treatment.20,21

Clinician Safety

CoolSculpting® specialists have undergone in-depth training by Allergan (Thailand) Limited.

Device Safety

CoolSculpting® systems are controlled cooling devices.2 If sensors detect that the skin is getting too cold, the system will shut down automatically.2

Shape your Body

The CoolSculpting® fat-freezing procedure reduces fat cells in different areas around the body.2,3,8-19

  • ABDOMEN6,12
  • FLANKS/SIDES14,18
  • DOUBLE CHIN7,11
  • THIGH8,13,15,17
  • BRA FAT12
  • UPPER ARMS10,15,16

From Pop Ices to CoolSculpting®

Scientists at Harvard University observed that some children who ate pop ices got dimples in their cheeks.26 This led Dieter Manstein, MD and R. Rox Anderson, MD, to realise that ice can selectively damage small pockets of fat cells.27

This idea that cold can target fat cells—without damaging the skin or surrounding tissue—was the insight behind cryolipolysis, the science on which the CoolSculpting® procedure is based.8,27
